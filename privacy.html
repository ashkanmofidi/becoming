Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Becoming... ("we," "our," or "the Service") is operated by Ash Mofidi. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store the following from your Google account:

Account Data: Your name, email address, and profile photo. This is used solely for authentication, displaying your identity within the app, and communicating with you about the Service.

When you use the Service, we also collect:

Usage Data: Focus session records (start time, duration, task name, category, completion status), timer settings preferences, and font size preferences. This data is generated by your activity and stored to provide you with analytics and progress tracking.

We do not collect browsing history, device identifiers, IP addresses for tracking purposes, or any data beyond what is described above. IP addresses may be temporarily processed for rate limiting and security purposes but are not stored long-term.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to: authenticate your identity and maintain your session; display your profile within the app; store and sync your timer settings and session history across devices; provide analytics dashboards and progress reports; send you notifications about completed focus sessions (with your permission); and respond to your feedback or support requests.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Vercel's cloud infrastructure (Vercel KV, a Redis-based key-value store) located in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit via TLS/HTTPS. Session cookies are HTTP-only, Secure, and SameSite=Strict. Authentication tokens use HMAC-SHA256. API endpoints are rate-limited to prevent abuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

Your data is processed only by the following service providers (subprocessors) as necessary to operate the Service: Google (authentication via OAuth 2.0) and Vercel Inc. (hosting and data storage). These providers have their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

5. Your Rights

Access: Export all your data at any time via Settings > Export CSV or JSON Backup.

Portability: Download your complete data in machine-readable format (JSON).

Deletion: Delete your entire account and all associated data via Settings > Delete Account. This action is permanent and removes your user record, all session history, and all settings from our servers.

Correction: Your profile information comes from Google and updates automatically when you update your Google account.

6. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect (fully described in Section 1). You may request deletion of your personal information using the Delete Account feature. We do not sell your personal information and have no plans to do so. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ashkan.mofidi@gmail.com.

7. European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, our lawful basis for processing your data is your consent (provided when you sign in with Google and accept our Terms). You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account. Your data is transferred to and stored in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication. We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party tracking cookies. The cookies we set are: bm_sid (session authentication token, HTTP-only, Secure, 30-day expiry) and bm_uid (user identifier for session validation, HTTP-only, Secure, 30-day expiry).

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all data is permanently removed immediately. Inactive accounts (no login for 12 months) may be purged with 30 days email notice.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes, by displaying a notice within the app.
